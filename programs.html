How We Work

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)

Accountability, evidence, and learning guide every stage of our programs.

The Osagyefo Ampem Foundation is committed to results-based programming and continuous learning to ensure accountability and measurable impact. We apply a Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL) framework that uses clear performance indicators, baseline data, and beneficiary feedback to track progress and assess outcomes. Monitoring is conducted on an ongoing basis, while formal evaluations are carried out biannually to measure program effectiveness and community impact.

Partners and donors receive regular progress updates, biannual reports, and participate in annual project review meetings where achievements, challenges, and lessons learned are discussed. Insights from our MEL processes guide program improvement and inform future initiatives.
