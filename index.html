Discover Our Story

Advancing Opportunity For Every Native Of Wenchiman




Having ascended the Wenchi Black Stool, Osagyefo Ampem Anye Amoapong Tabrako III leads a unifying agenda that rallies Wenchiman around shared development and growth.






Osagyefo Ampem Foundation transforms this mandate into sustainable programs that uplift education, health care, water and sanitation, entrepreneurship, tourism and heritage, and technology across the Wenchi Traditional Area.

Education

Academic Excellence Scholarships keep brilliant yet financially challenged students in school, Wheels of Change gets rural learners to class, we strengthen educational infrastructure, and we spark excitement through learning activities and competitions.

Health Care

We partner with facilities to improve health infrastructure, deliver education on infectious diseases, malaria, and teenage pregnancy, and extend health insurance support so vulnerable families can access dignified care.

Water & Sanitation

Provision of potable water brings safe sources to rural communities, while sanitation campaigns mobilise residents to protect the environment and reduce water-borne diseases.

Entrepreneurship

We inspire youth in Wenchiman to launch enterprises, offer coaching and skill-building, and provide targeted support that helps existing businesses increase productivity and create jobs.

Tourism, Culture & Heritage

Festivals, cultural showcases, and school competitions celebrate the rich heritage of Wenchiman while positioning Wenchi, the Bono Region, and Ghana as vibrant tourism destinations.

Technology

Partnerships with technology organisations deliver training, STEM and coding programs, and ICT equipment that equip the youth of Wenchiman with future-ready skills.

Vision & Mission

Osagyefo Ampem Foundation seeks to improve the lives of Wenchiman and accelerate the development of the Wenchi Traditional Area through impactful programs, services, initiatives, and projects.
