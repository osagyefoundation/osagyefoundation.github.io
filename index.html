About Us

The Osagyefo Ampem Foundation is a community-focused non-profit organization dedicated to advancing sustainable development within the Wenchi Traditional Area of Ghana.

Inspired by the vision and leadership of His Royal Majesty, Osagyefo Ampem Anye Amoapong Tabrako III, the Foundation was established to promote unity, development, and shared prosperity among the people of Wenchiman. Our work focuses on improving livelihoods, strengthening human capital, and unlocking the socio-economic potential of the community through inclusive and locally driven development initiatives.

Osagyefo Ampem Foundation was established and legally incorporated as a non-profit Foundation in Ghana on 19th September 2024, in commemoration of the fifth anniversary of the enstoolment of His Royal Majesty, Osagyefo Ampem Anye Amoapong Tabrako III, as Paramount Chief of the Wenchi Traditional Area.

Our Programmatic Focus Areas

We advance sustainable development through six integrated pillars tailored to community priorities across the Wenchi Traditional Area.

Education

We promote access to quality education and improved learning outcomes through:

  • Academic Excellence Scholarship Program for brilliant but needy students
  • Support for school infrastructure development
  • Educational activities and academic competitions

Health Care

We work to improve community health and access to basic healthcare services through:

  • Support for health infrastructure improvement
  • Community health education campaigns on malaria, HIV/AIDS, STIs, teenage pregnancy, and other prevalent health issues
  • NHIS enrollment supports vulnerable and underserved populations

Water & Sanitation

We promote healthy communities by improving access to clean water and sanitation:

  • Borehole and potable water systems for rural communities
  • Environmental sanitation and hygiene education campaigns

Entrepreneurship & Youth Empowerment

We support economic empowerment and job creation through:

  • Youth enterprise training programs
  • Business mentorship and productivity support
  • Linkages to financing opportunities and markets

Technology & Innovation

We bridge the digital divide and prepare youth for the future through:

  • STEM and coding programs and competitions
  • Provision of ICT laboratories and equipment
  • Partnerships with technology institutions and companies

Tourism, Culture & Heritage

We preserve and promote culture while unlocking tourism potential through:

  • Cultural festivals and heritage promotion initiatives
  • School-based cultural competitions
  • Tourism development advocacy within Wenchi, the Bono Region, and Ghana
Partnerships & Sustainability

The Foundation works in collaboration with:

  • Traditional Authorities
  • Government Agencies
  • Development Partners
  • Private Sector and CSR Programs
  • Civil Society Organizations

We prioritize local ownership, strategic partnerships, and sustainable, income-generating approaches to ensure long-term impact and community resilience.
